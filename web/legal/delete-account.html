Data & Account Deletion

Life Stations · Last updated: 19 July 2026

Delete inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open Life Stations and go to the Me tab.
  2. Tap Settings & safety.
  3. Tap Data & account deletion.
  4. Tap Delete my account & data and confirm.

What gets deleted

  • Your private account record (name, city, language, phone, WhatsApp number)
  • All listing cards you published in every domain
  • Your uploaded photos
  • Likes, blocks, share links, and push-notification tokens
  • Your sign-in account (you will be signed out everywhere)

What may be retained

Safety reports filed against or by your account may be retained in anonymized form for moderation and legal compliance. Crash and analytics data is anonymized and expires automatically.

Request deletion without the app

If you have uninstalled the app, email the support address listed on our Google Play store listing from a message that includes the phone number you registered with. We verify ownership of the number before deleting. Requests are completed within 30 days.
