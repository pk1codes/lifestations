Privacy Policy

Life Stations · Last updated: 19 July 2026

What this app does

Life Stations is a discovery app for five life domains — Marriage, Jobs, Rooms, Bikes, and Home Help. You keep one private account and publish independent, privacy-redacted listing cards per domain.

Information we collect

  • Account: phone number (for OTP sign-in), display name, city, native language, and a WhatsApp contact number you choose to add.
  • Listings: the text and photos you publish for a domain.
  • Activity: likes, blocks, reports, and share links you create.
  • Device: push-notification tokens, crash logs, and anonymized analytics events.
  • Trust flags: self-attested labels (for example "ID+"). We do not collect or store government ID documents.

How your information is used

  • Discovery cards show only redacted, non-contact information.
  • Your phone and WhatsApp numbers stay in a private vault and are revealed to another user only after mutual interest in the same domain and a verified phone session on both sides.
  • Push tokens send match and safety notifications.
  • Analytics and crash data keep the app reliable.

Where data is stored

Data is processed and stored on Google Firebase (Firestore database, Cloud Storage for photos, Firebase Authentication, Cloud Messaging, Analytics, and Crashlytics). Uploaded photos are compressed and screened by automated moderation before they appear publicly.

What we never do

We do not sell your personal data. We do not show your phone number on any public card. We do not use your photos for anything other than the listings you created.

Sharing

Public share links (/c/…) contain only the redacted card you chose to share — never contact details. Data processors are limited to the Google Firebase services listed above.

Retention & deletion

Your data is kept while your account is active. You can delete your account and data at any time from Settings → Data & account deletion inside the app, or request deletion at this page. Deletion requests are completed within 30 days.

Age requirement

Life Stations is for adults aged 18 and above. Underage profiles are removed and can be reported from any card via Report → underage / child safety.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the support email listed on our Google Play store listing.
